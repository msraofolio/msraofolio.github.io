# Case Study: Troubleshooting Guide

## Overview
A structured approach to diagnosing and resolving common operational issues.

**Audience:** Support engineers and operators  
**Product/Feature:** Operational tooling  
**Documentation Type:** Troubleshooting guide

---

## Problem
Outline frequent failure scenarios, unclear error messages, and missing runbooks.

---

## Approach
- Mapped common error patterns to root causes
- Created reproducible steps and checks
- Added quick-fix actions and escalation paths

---

## Solution
Stepwise diagnostic flows, command examples, and escalation checklists.

---

## Impact
- Faster mean time to resolution (MTTR)
- Clear escalation criteria for support teams

---

## Sample Excerpt
1. Check service status
2. Review recent deployments
3. Collect logs and trace IDs
